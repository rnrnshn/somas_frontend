Design the Savings Module for the platform:

SOMAS – Social Mass Payments & Savings Platform

This module enables administrators to manage savings programs linked to social transfer campaigns.

The module must support:
	•	Savings campaigns
	•	Beneficiary participation
	•	Savings deposits tracking
	•	Savings analytics

The design must be:
	•	Simple
	•	Elegant
	•	Institutional
	•	Data-focused

Follow Shadcn Design System principles.

Savings Campaign List

Savings Participants

Savings Analytics Dashboard
