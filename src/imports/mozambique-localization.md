Localizar Backoffice para Moçambique

Update the entire SOMAS Backoffice interface to be localized for Mozambique.

The platform is used to manage social transfer programs and financial assistance distributed to beneficiaries across Mozambican provinces.

All interface labels, fields, and operational terminology must be adapted to the Mozambican context.

The interface language must be Portuguese (Mozambique).

Avoid Brazilian or overly formal European Portuguese.

Use simple, operational language suitable for NGOs, government programs, and field operators.
